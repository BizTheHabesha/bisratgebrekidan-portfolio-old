About Me

Hello! My name is Biz, and I'm working to become a full stack developer. As of now, I'm proffcient in Lua, C++, and C# and have deployed in each in at least one project. But enough about my skills, you've (hopefully) already seen my skills in the projects section. My full name is Bisrat Gebrekidan and I was born in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. Me and my cousin Sammy have been playing computer games since I came to America in 2006, and I inevitably picked up game development at a young age. My first language was Lua, which I used in Roblox Studio to make several games. By the time I was in high school, I had begun C# and unity. All throughout my life my family told me that I was meant to be an engineer, and they told me to take inspiration from his mechanical engineering background. It wasn't until I started applying to university that I realized I could turn coding into a career in its own right. Three years later, and I'm three quarters through my degree and pursuing a bootcamp certificate as well.
